  • Prepare a nice meal

    • To prepare a meal, take the following steps: Shop for ingredients and plan your menu. Grow your own herbs or buy them at a farmers market, if possible. Cook your foods as follows: meats and fish should be seared quickly on high heat to give them color and flavor; pasta should be boiled in plenty of water until al dente, then drained; root vegetables like carrots should be stirred in plenty of butter or olive oil while they are still hot so they soak up the butter.

  • The hottest peppers

    • The Carolina Reaper is currently the hottest pepper in the world. It's been measured at more than 2 million Scoville units, making it about 400 times hotter than a jalapeño. It was created in South Carolina by Ed Currie, who discovered it when a crossbreeding error gave him a uniquely potent pepper.

    At our restaurant we are constantly trying to find ways to make our menu items be more appealing to the customers.
